El nostre campament

Tenim la sort de viure uns dies en un lloc tan especial com aquest, envoltats de fagedes, pins i avets, i al costat d'un gorg espectacular on ens podem banyar i refrescar-nos. Un lloc en calma i tranquil·litat on ens visiten alguns animals i ens adormim sentint el so del riu. Un espai on desconnectar, relaxar-nos i carregar-nos d'energia…

Enrollment form

To come to Natkam you must fill out the registration form below. Once filled out and sent, se it will redirect you to the page where you can make the 40% payment by bank card or Bizum and you will have your place reserved. You will then receive a confirmation email where we will inform you of all the steps to follow. Start by selecting the number of participants and when you choose the age group you will be shown the available shifts to come to Natkam.


Information age groups:

Children: born from 2014 to 2017.

Media: born from 2011 to 2013.

Youth: born from 2006 to 2010.
